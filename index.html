Overview

When: TBA   |   Where: RoboSoft 2026   |   Duration: Half Day

Reproducibility remains a major challenge in soft robotics due to variability in materials, fabrication processes, experimental protocols, and simulation assumptions. This workshop brings together researchers to discuss challenges and practical approaches for improving reproducibility across the soft robotics pipeline.

Why Attend?

  • Learn best practices for reproducible fabrication and testing
  • Understand limitations and validation strategies in soft robot simulation
  • Engage with researchers shaping reproducibility standards
